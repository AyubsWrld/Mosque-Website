Sahaba Youth Centre, Incoming Hub for the Youth

We're excited to announce that the Sahaba Youth Centre (SYC) will officially be opening in the new year inshAllah! This new facility will serve as a vibrant hub for the community's youth, offering Quran and Arabic classes, a basketball court, a lounge area, and a dedicated homework club.

Sahaba Mosque: A Legacy

Sahaba Mosque has been a vital part of the downtown Muslim community in Edmonton, Alberta, for 26 years. It serves as a spiritual center, offering daily prayers, educational programs, and events that foster unity and faith. Over the years, the mosque has become a trusted institution, providing a welcoming space for worship and connection.

Annual Conferences

Sahaba Mosque holds annual conferences on various Islamic topics, bringing together scholars and community members to deepen their understanding of the faith. These events foster learning, discussion, and unity, offering insights into spiritual growth and contemporary issues through an Islamic lens.

Edmonton Brothers Basketball

EBB unites Edmonton’s Muslim community through a shared passion for basketball. The league provides a space for friendly competition, teamwork, and personal growth, fostering stronger bonds within the community. By combining sports and brotherhood, EBB promotes unity, fitness, and a sense of belonging.

Empowering the Next Generation

Sahaba Mosque has made a profound impact on the young adult community by providing programs and spaces tailored to their spiritual and social growth. Through youth-focused events, educational initiatives, and mentorship opportunities, it empowers young Muslims to navigate life with confidence and faith.
